Our aim is to explore how image segmentation can be applied in the medical field- specifically human skin to detect any abnormality and to identify its disease. This is done through pixel classification using the segmentation by Machine Learning. After training the neural network model, we would test it on real life subjects to assess and validate our results and implement our product in the medical field.
